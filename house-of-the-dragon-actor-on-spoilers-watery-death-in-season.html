‘House of the Dragon’ Actor on [SPOILER]’s Watery Death in Season 3 Premiere: It Was ‘Challenging Trying to Keep My Eyes Open’

United States · 2026-06-22

The Brutal Reality Behind Jace’s Watery Death in ‘House of the Dragon’ Season 3

Fans of *House of the Dragon* are still reeling from the Season 3 premiere, which delivered one of the most harrowing scenes in the series so far: the drowning death of Jacaerys Velaryon during the Battle of the Gullet. The moment was not only emotionally devastating but physically grueling for the actor, who recently revealed that filming the underwater sequence was “challenging trying to keep my eyes open” for multiple takes. It’s a detail that underscores just how much went into creating this pivotal, heartbreaking moment.

The Irony of Rhaenyra’s “No Further Bloodshed” Promise

The death of Jace carries a particularly bitter irony. Throughout the first two seasons, Rhaenyra Targaryen repeatedly swore to avoid further bloodshed, positioning herself as the more restrained claimant to the Iron Throne. Yet the Season 3 premiere opens with the Battle of the Gullet, a naval clash that tragically drowns her own son first. This narrative twist amplifies the thematic weight of the conflict: even the most sincere intentions can be swallowed by the tides of war. The actor’s difficulty keeping his eyes open during the shoot mirrors the literal struggle for survival his character faced, making the scene feel unbearably real.

Behind the Scenes of the Battle of the Gullet

Filming a drowning scene required more than just acting talent. The actor described multiple underwater takes, each demanding complete immersion—literally and figuratively. Keeping eyes open in saltwater while simulating panic and exhaustion proved physically taxing. Yet this dedication paid off, creating a visceral, haunting sequence that leaves viewers breathless. The Battle of the Gullet is not just a turning point in the war; it’s a stark reminder that no character is safe, and no promise can protect against the chaos of conflict.

A Death That Redefines the Season

Jace’s watery death in the *House of the Dragon* Season 3 premiere is more than a shocking moment—it’s the catalyst that will reshape Rhaenyra’s resolve and the entire war to come. The actor’s commitment to authenticity, from the challenge of keeping his eyes open to the emotional toll of the scene, ensures this loss will linger. As the season unfolds, fans will likely see how this drowning ignites a fire that no one can quench.
